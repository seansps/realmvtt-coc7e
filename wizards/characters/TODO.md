Effects are campaign-level records (not in the ruleset). You'll need to configure settings.effects in your realmdb.rulesets.json with the modifier types above. I provided the full JSON structure in my earlier message.

Suggested effect records to create: Unconscious, Dying, Dead, Major Wound, Prone, Temporary Insanity, Indefinite Insanity, Permanent Insanity, Outnumbered, Point Blank, Aiming, Surprised, Darkness, Restrained.
